Robust and Interpretable Early Triaging Support system (RIETS)

About RIETS: Developed based on extensive data from geographically distributed 19 hospitals in South Korea over a three-year pandemic period, RIETS is a machine learning-based prognostic model aimed at early patient triaging for COVID-19 severity. Incorporating 11 readily available features upon hospitalization, it provides risk estimates and detailed interpretations, assisting in clinical decision-making. RIETS is recognized for its robust discriminative power (AUC of 0.937 [0.935-0.938], novel interpretability (DDRTree - see results page), and high potential for clinical applicability.
