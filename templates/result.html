About DDRTree (discriminative dimensionality reduction via learning a tree): A prevalent genomic analysis tool, DDRTree is an unsupervised learning technique that efficiently visualizes multi-dimensional features into a two-dimensional tree structure. In RIETS, DDRTree was applied to cluster and characterize each patient. This method is known for its high stability and accuracy in capturing the cluster information.
